About
From plan to photograph

I studied architecture and urban planning at the Universität Stuttgart, finishing in 2025. Somewhere in the middle of drawing plans and building models, I got more interested in how a building actually looks — the way light lands on a façade at a certain hour, or how a material reads differently in a photograph than on a site visit.

I'm now building a photography practice around that interest, with a particular focus on architecture. This site will grow as the work does — for now, consider it a working sketch of where that's headed.
